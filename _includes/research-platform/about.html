/ About the research platform

The Research Platform, an online hub, aims to connect students and research leaders in ML/AI. Students can discover research labs with projects suited to their interests, while research leaders showcase their work and offer collaboration opportunities. The platform fosters collaboration and increases accessibility to research opportunities for students, ultimately driving innovation and creating a positive impact in the field of machine learning and beyond. Are you the research leader, and you want to add your project on our website? Great, go to our Research platform github, make the PR as explained in the tutorial, wait for our acceptance and that is it! We are looking forward for your submissions.
